# Synthesis Protocol

## Purpose
Turn disagreement into useful editorial intelligence.

## Final Synthesis Must Include

### 1. What The Book Is
A precise description of the book as an aesthetic object.

### 2. What The Book Thinks It Is
The implied self-concept of the manuscript.

### 3. What The Book Actually Does Best
Concrete strengths, ranked.

### 4. What The Book Most Risks
Concrete weaknesses, ranked.

### 5. Recursive Structure Verdict
Does the full-book architecture justify local delays, ambiguities, repetitions, asymmetries, or tonal strangeness?

### 6. Character Arc Verdict
Which arcs are earned, which are over-protected, which need sharper dramatization?

### 7. Payoff Audit
For each major payoff:
- setup quality
- escalation quality
- delivery quality
- emotional credibility
- whether hindsight strengthens the book

### 8. Reader Experience Forecast
How different reader types may respond.

### 9. Revision Plan
Prioritized by:
- high-impact structural issues
- medium-impact character/prose issues
- low-impact polish issues

### 10. Dissent Reports
Any critic whose position cannot be merged into consensus gets a preserved minority report.

## Rule
A useful synthesis does not erase disagreement. It tells the author what the disagreement means.
