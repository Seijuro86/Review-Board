# Independent Review Protocol

Each critic must write independently after full-book ingestion and before reading the other critics.

## Required Review Format

```markdown
# [Critic Name] Independent Full-Book Review

## Central Thesis

## What The Book Is Trying To Do

## Where It Most Succeeds

## Where It Most Fails Or Risks Failure

## Recursive Structure Assessment

## Character Arc Assessment

## Prose / Style / Form Assessment

## Emotional Payoff Assessment

## Strongest Evidence
Use specific scenes, quoted lines, or described moments.

## Hostile Reading
What would a skeptical reader say?

## Defense Of The Book
What is the strongest argument in the book's favor?

## One Revision Priority
```

## Rules

- Do not imitate the real critic’s exact prose.
- Use the synthesized lens, not impersonation.
- Be specific.
- Do not defer to author intent.
- Do not summarize the plot except when needed as evidence.
- Judge the full-book experience.
